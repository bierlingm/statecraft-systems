Yakima Valley Supply & Labor is a wholesale distributor of safety equipment and jobsite consumables based in Prosser, Washington, serving farms, remediation contractors, and working crews across Central Washington and the Inland Northwest. Our goal is simple: reliable products at practical prices for the businesses that go through them on the job.

We work directly with manufacturers and maintain a lean distribution model so we can offer many commonly used jobsite supplies at competitive pricing compared to large national distributors. We work with businesses of all sizes. Rather than maintaining a massive catalog, we focus on procuring the supplies that working crews regularly go through on the job.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Yakima Valley Supply sell?
Yakima Valley Supply & Labor sells wholesale safety equipment and jobsite consumables — disposable coveralls, nitrile gloves, ANSI Z87+ safety glasses, respirators, paint and prep supplies, abrasives, adhesives, and tape — to farms, remediation contractors, painters, and construction crews.
Where is Yakima Valley Supply located?
Yakima Valley Supply & Labor LLC is based in Prosser, Washington, and serves customers throughout Central Washington and the Inland Northwest.
Who runs Yakima Valley Supply?
The company is run by Tyler Sabin, who works directly with growers, contractors, and crew leads to source the supplies their teams use up on the job.
How do I order from Yakima Valley Supply?
New customers fill out the industrial customer intake form, then a sales representative follows up by phone or email to confirm products, quantities, and delivery. Returning customers reorder by emailing orders@yakimavalleysupply.com or calling (425) 999-2904.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes. Yakima Valley Supply works primarily with businesses purchasing for crew use; bulk pricing is available depending on order quantity and product type.
Which industries does Yakima Valley Supply serve?
Agriculture (orchards, vineyards, hop yards), mold remediation, asbestos abatement, painting and coatings, industrial cleaning, and general construction. See the industries page for what each industry typically goes through.
Do you deliver, or is this pickup only?
Yakima Valley Supply coordinates delivery for orders within its Central Washington / Inland Northwest service area; specifics are set during the intake call.
