1. The two records to add

At your registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, wherever the domain is parked), add:

TypeNameValue
CNAME@yakimavalleysupply-website.pages.dev
CNAMEwwwyakimavalleysupply-website.pages.dev

2. If your registrar refuses CNAME at the apex

Some registrars don't allow CNAME on @. Use these proxied records instead — Cloudflare will route them automatically:

TypeNameValue
A@192.0.2.1
AAAA@100::
CNAMEwwwyakimavalleysupply-website.pages.dev

3. Fallback: switch to Cloudflare nameservers

If neither approach works at your registrar, the cleanest move is to switch the domain to Cloudflare's nameservers. Then everything — DNS, cert, routing — is automatic. Reply to the confirmation email and we'll walk you through it.

What happens next

  • You add the records above (or send us the registrar login if you'd rather we do it).
  • DNS propagates — usually within an hour, sometimes up to 24h.
  • Cloudflare auto-issues the SSL cert.
  • Site is live at yakimavalleysupply.com.
