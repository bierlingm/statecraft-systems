How to Leave Feedback

A small feedback widget is loaded on every page — you'll see a floating marker in the lower corner. You don't need an account. You don't need to track changes in a document. Just click the thing you want to change and type.

  1. Open any page from the list above.
  2. Click the element you want to comment on — a sentence, a button, a section header. The widget remembers exactly what you clicked.
  3. Leave a note. Blunt is good. Rough is good. "This word is wrong." "Move this up." "Never say that about our customers." Your name is optional.
  4. That's it. Your comments come straight back to me. I'll review them, reply if needed, and revise the draft.

This is faster than redlining a Word doc, especially for visual things. If you prefer to just call or email instead, the contact info is at the bottom of this page and at the end of every draft page.
