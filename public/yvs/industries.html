Industries We Serve

Supplies for Where the Work Actually Happens

Yakima Valley Supply is built around the supplies crews use up on the job — the items that get thrown away, worn out, or replaced every few days. We focus on where work actually happens, and where materials get consumed the fastest.

Agriculture

In orchard and farm operations, protective gear gets used hard and replaced often — especially during spray and sanitation work.

We support crews during

  • Pesticide and nutrient spray applications
  • Mixing and loading chemicals
  • Equipment cleanup and sanitation
  • Fieldwork in dusty or wet conditions

Common consumables

  • Disposable coveralls (often changed daily or per spray)
  • Nitrile gloves (frequent swaps during mixing and loading)
  • Safety glasses
  • Spray crew protection gear

Mold Remediation

On remediation jobs, PPE is used once and discarded. Every containment setup burns through gear quickly.

We support crews during

  • Containment setup and tear-down
  • Removal of contaminated drywall, insulation, and debris
  • Cleaning and antimicrobial application
  • Work inside negative-air environments

Common consumables

  • Protective coveralls (disposed of after each job or entry)
  • Nitrile gloves (multiple changes per shift)
  • Safety glasses
  • General jobsite consumables

Asbestos Abatement

Abatement work requires strict disposal of PPE after exposure. Crews go through a high volume of protective gear on every project.

We support crews during

  • Containment and sealing of work areas
  • Removal of asbestos-containing materials
  • Bagging and disposal procedures
  • Decontamination processes

Common consumables

  • Disposable protective coveralls (single-use in many cases)
  • Gloves
  • Safety glasses
  • Jobsite supplies used during abatement

Painting & Coatings

Painting crews constantly cycle through tools and prep materials. Supplies wear out, get saturated, or are discarded between jobs.

We support crews during

  • Masking and prep work before painting
  • Rolling and brushing walls, trim, and finishes
  • Cleanup and job turnover
  • Multi-day or multi-phase paint projects

Common consumables

  • Paint brushes (worn out or job-specific use)
  • Rollers and roller covers (replaced frequently)
  • Masking tape and masking film
  • Disposable coveralls
  • Nitrile gloves

Industrial Cleaning & Maintenance

Cleaning crews regularly work in environments where protective gear is contaminated or degraded and needs to be replaced.

We support crews during

  • Deep cleaning and sanitation projects
  • Dust, debris, and contamination removal
  • Maintenance in industrial facilities
  • Post-job cleanup operations

Common consumables

  • Disposable coveralls
  • Nitrile gloves (frequent changes)
  • Safety glasses
  • General jobsite consumables

Construction & Contracting

Across construction sites, crews rely on basic protective gear and supplies that get used up throughout the job cycle.

We support crews during

  • Demolition and debris removal
  • Finishing work and jobsite prep
  • Specialty projects requiring protective gear
  • General day-to-day site work

Common consumables

  • Protective coveralls
  • Gloves
  • Safety glasses
  • Masking tape and jobsite supplies
