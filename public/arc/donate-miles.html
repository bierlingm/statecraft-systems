Common Questions

Are donated miles tax-deductible?
The IRS does not currently recognize frequent flyer mile donations as tax-deductible contributions. However, monetary donations to ARC are fully deductible as ARC is a registered 501(c)(3).
Is there a minimum donation?
Minimums are set by each airline, typically 500 to 1,000 miles. Every bit helps — a single domestic rescue flight can require 25,000+ miles.
How are the miles used?
Miles fund flights for ARC's field operators — former CIA, military special ops, and law enforcement professionals — who travel domestically and internationally to recover missing and abducted children.
What if my airline isn't listed?
Email ARC at baz@recoveryofchildren.com with your airline and available miles. They'll work with you to arrange the transfer.
Can I donate hotel or credit card points?
Yes. Many hotel loyalty programs (Marriott Bonvoy, Hilton Honors, IHG) and credit card reward programs allow charity transfers. Contact ARC to coordinate.
