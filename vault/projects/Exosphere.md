# Exosphere

Multi-week to two-month training intensives focused on entrepreneurship, creative institution-building, and the living spirit. Founded by [[Skinner Layne]].

## Significance for Statecraft Systems

A key part of Moritz's [[Formation]] lineage. Direct experience of conceiving, organizing, and running intensive programs — not just as a participant but as an organizer and teacher.

Exosphere represents the tradition of "the living spirit and creative institution-building" — creating environments where people transform through immersive, shared experience rather than through instruction alone.

## What it demonstrated

- That multi-week intensive formats can produce genuine transformation
- That [[Formation]] requires real shared challenge, not just curriculum
- The practical skills of program design, logistics, and facilitation at scale
