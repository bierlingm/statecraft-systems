# Statecraft Systems

The venture itself. The legal-corporate-financial vehicle through which Moritz contracts, offers products and services, and organizes the broader ecosystem.

## What it is

Not a consulting practice. A node in [[The Web]] — a growing pattern of ventures, people, commons, and tools governed by [[Reciprocity]].

## What it does

Helps founders, executives, and their teams become genuinely capable — technically, structurally, and personally — of creating the outcomes they are after. Two inseparable dimensions:
- **Technical capacity** — making people [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive|AI-native]]
- **Structural capacity** — helping people see and reshape the structures they operate within

## The thesis

[[The Statecraft Thesis]]: Desirable states — for individuals, ventures, and civilizations — are created through the craft of building viable structures.
