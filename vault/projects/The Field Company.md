# The Field Company

Experiential programs for developing clarity, character, and the capacity for responsible action. Website: fieldcompany.org

## What it is

Small, curated retreats combining physical challenge, structured dialogue, and exposure to nature. Designed for men to practice cooperative decision-making, examine themselves, and build lasting peer relationships.

## Design philosophy

Draws on historical patterns of [[Formation]] — field camps, salons, academies — expressed in contemporary, minimalist form. Each program is small, selective, and emphasizes contribution over consumption. Participants are collaborators in the experience.

## Role in the ecosystem

The formation arm of [[The Web]]. Where ideas are tested in practice, character is made visible, and trust is developed through shared work. Complements [[Modern Minuteman]]'s structured discipline training with experiential, embodied formation.
