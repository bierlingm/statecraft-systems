# Capable Few

Website: capablefew.com

A [[Statecraft Systems]] production highlighting serious people — those who meet the standard of capability, commitment, and reciprocal loyalty that the ecosystem values.

## Significance

Not a client project or "result" — it is an expression of the [[Counter-Elite]] aspiration. A way of making visible the kind of people the work produces and attracts.

The name itself carries the thesis: the few who are capable, and the aspiration to grow their number through [[Formation]] and genuine challenge.
