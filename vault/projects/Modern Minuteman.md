# Modern Minuteman

Training and [[Formation]] for civic resilience. Website: modernminuteman.net

## What it is

The first product created under [[Statecraft Systems]]. A structured cohort program teaching four disciplines from [[Natural Law]]:
1. **Testimony**
2. **[[Decidability]]**
3. **Cooperation**
4. **Statecraft** (taught by Moritz; the other three by Fellows of the [[Natural Law Institute]])

## History

- Two month-long cohorts conducted
- Currently in a holding pattern — rethinking the future of the project
- Intent is to move into an ongoing structure

## Significance

Modern Minuteman is the clearest expression of the [[Formation]] dimension of [[Statecraft Systems]]' work: training groups of people in the skills to be effective operators in the environment we are in.

It represents the same pattern as the AI-native work — enabling people to become capable of things they weren't before — applied to civic and structural literacy rather than technical tools.

A primary node. Chief among Statecraft Systems' own offerings. Represents the formation/training dimension of the ecosystem.
