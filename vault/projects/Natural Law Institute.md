# Natural Law Institute

The institution associated with [[Curt Doolittle]]'s development of [[Natural Law]].

Three Fellows of the Institute taught Testimony, [[Decidability]], and Cooperation in the original [[Modern Minuteman]] program. Moritz taught Statecraft.

The Institute represents the institutional form of the Natural Law body of work — the community of practitioners and scholars working on decidability, reciprocity, and the conditions for viable cooperation.
