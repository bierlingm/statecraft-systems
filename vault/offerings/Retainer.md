# Retainer

$2-4K/month. Keep current as the landscape shifts.

## What it is

Ongoing relationship with teams/companies that have been through [[Discovery]], [[Build]], and [[Train]]. Ensures they stay current as AI tools, workflows, and the broader landscape evolve.

## Why it matters

The landscape moves fast. What was built three months ago may need updating. New tools emerge. New patterns become possible. The retainer relationship prevents the slow slide back to AI-naive operations ([[Oscillation]]) and keeps the team at the frontier.

## Structural role

The retainer is the team equivalent of the [[Sparring Partner]] for individuals. It maintains the relationship and the capability over time.

## Natural flow

[[Discovery]] → [[Build]] → [[Train]] → **Retainer**
