# Discovery

$3-5K. The entry point for the team/company track.

## What it is

Survey the current state of the organization's workflows, tools, and structures. Map opportunities for AI-native transformation and structural improvement. Produce a clear picture of where things are and where they should go.

## Methodology

This is [[Structural Tension]] applied at the organizational level: extreme clarity about current reality and desired outcome, with an honest assessment of the gap.

## Critical requirement

Must have the backing of the relevant decision-maker. This is why [[Statecraft Systems]] works directly with CEOs and founders — or in the context of teams, with the leader of that team. Without decision-maker commitment, discovery produces documents that nobody acts on.

## Natural flow

Typically follows the CEO's personal experience ([[Unlock Session]], [[Personal Stack Build]]). Once they see personal value, they bring Moritz in for the team.

**Discovery** → [[Build]] → [[Train]] → [[Retainer]]
