# Survey

$3-5K. The entry point for the team/company track.

## What it is

Mapping current reality against desired outcomes. Establishing [[Structural Tension|structural tension]] at the organizational level: extreme clarity about where things are and where they must go.

## Methodology

Applied structural tension — current reality on one side, desired outcome on the other, with an honest assessment of the gap between them.

## Critical requirement

Must have the backing of the relevant decision-maker. This is why [[Statecraft Systems]] works directly with CEOs and founders — or in the context of teams, with the leader of that team. Without decision-maker commitment, survey produces documents that nobody acts on.

## Natural flow

Typically follows the CEO's personal experience ([[Entry]], [[Infrastructure]]). Once they see personal value, they bring Moritz in for the team.

**Survey** → [[Construction]] → [[Drill]] → [[Continuity]]
