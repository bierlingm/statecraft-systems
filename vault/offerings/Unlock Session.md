# Unlock Session

~3 hours / $1,500. The entry point for individuals.

## What happens

Audit the person's workflow, set up tools on their devices, produce immediate payoff. This is the moment someone crosses from [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive|AI-naive]] to AI-native — they see their workflow as manipulable infrastructure for the first time.

## What actually transfers

Depends on who they are and what their situation is. Could be any of:
- They see their workflow as manipulable infrastructure
- They experience being unblocked by a tool that actually works
- They realize the power differential between AI-native and AI-naive
- They see the structure they are in and how it [[Oscillation|oscillates]] or advances
- They learn to set up [[Structural Tension]] — clarity on what they want vs. what reality looks like

Sometimes the unlock is discovering that the thing they thought should be done shouldn't be done at all, was ill-framed, or was improperly situated in the larger structure it is supposed to support.

## Structural role

The natural starting point for the individual track. Personal value first, then → [[Personal Stack Build]] → [[Sparring Partner]]. Once the CEO sees personal value, they bring Moritz in for the team → [[Discovery]] → [[Build]] → [[Train]] → [[Retainer]].

## As formation

A compressed [[Formation]] experience. The hands-on, direct-contact nature of the session — building on their actual devices, with their actual workflows — is craft in the literal sense.
