# Drill

$2,500-5K. Formation through repetition under pressure.

## What it is

The [[Formation]] phase for teams. After [[Construction]] creates the tools and workflows, Drill ensures the team can operate, maintain, and improve them independently.

## Why this is separate from Construction

Building something and being able to use it are different capabilities. Drill closes the gap between "the tools exist" and "the team is [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive|AI-native]]." Without this phase, tools atrophy and the team reverts to old patterns ([[Oscillation]]).

## Natural flow

[[Survey]] → [[Construction]] → **Drill** → [[Continuity]]
