# Train

$2,500-5K. Teach the team to iterate on what was built.

## What it is

The [[Formation]] phase for teams. After [[Build]] creates the tools and workflows, Train ensures the team can operate, maintain, and improve them independently.

## Why this is separate from Build

Building something and being able to use it are different capabilities. Train closes the gap between "the tools exist" and "the team is [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive|AI-native]]." Without this phase, tools atrophy and the team reverts to old patterns ([[Oscillation]]).

## Natural flow

[[Discovery]] → [[Build]] → **Train** → [[Retainer]]
