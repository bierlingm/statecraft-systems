# sd-core

Structural Dynamics Core. The flagship tool of [[Statecraft Systems]].

## What it is

A framework for enabling people individually and cooperatively to create the outcomes they're after and support each other in doing so, while effectively using AI tools without getting lost in the weeds.

## Relationship to Structural Dynamics

The software/framework expression of [[Structural Dynamics]]. The name makes the connection explicit: Structural Dynamics Core.

## Current status

Not ready for a dedicated page on the website. Mentioned in the tools section alongside [[spikes.sh]], [[beats]], and [[werk-cli]].

## Significance

sd-core represents the convergence of the two dimensions of [[Statecraft Systems]]' work: structural capacity ([[Structural Dynamics]]) and technical capacity ([[AI-Native vs AI-Naive]]). It is the tool that embodies both.
