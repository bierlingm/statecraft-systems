# Noah Revoy

Founder of themetafather.com. One of the key examples of [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive]] transformation.

## The intervention

Moritz introduced Noah to Claude Code. The result:
- Noah immediately rebuilt his entire website/platform
- Moved his business off Kajabi (a pre-packaged platform) and created his own
- Now runs his business through tools he controls

## Why this matters

This is a textbook example of the sovereignty dimension of AI-native capability. Moving from a platform someone else controls (Kajabi) to infrastructure you built yourself is a concrete expression of [[Sovereignty]] — self-determination by self-determined means.

It is also an example of what a single [[Unlock Session]]-style intervention can produce: the moment someone sees their workflow as manipulable infrastructure, they cannot unsee it.
