# Robert Fritz

Creator of [[Structural Dynamics]]. Author and teacher of the creative process as a structural discipline.

## Key contributions

- The distinction between [[Creative Orientation]] and [[Reactive Orientation]]
- The concept of [[Structural Tension]] as the engine of creative advance
- The insight that structure determines the path of least resistance — and therefore determines behavior
- "[[Success That Succeeds]]" — outcomes that are structurally sound
- The understanding of [[Oscillation]] as a structural property, not a character flaw

## Relationship to Statecraft Systems

Fritz's work is the methodological foundation. [[Structural Dynamics]] is "the engine at the center of everything [[Statecraft Systems]] does."

Moritz's direct experience with structural dynamics came through coaching by [[Nicholas Catton]], who uses Fritz's discipline as the primary engine of forward movement. Moritz is pursuing the Foundations of Structural Thinking course and certification as a Structural Consultant.
