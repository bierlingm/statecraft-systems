# Miguel Angel Fernandez

Practitioner and teacher of [[Operative Traditions]].

## Relationship to Statecraft Systems

An influence on the worldview and methodology of [[Statecraft Systems]]. Fernandez's work on operative traditions contributes to the understanding that traditions are not merely inherited customs but active, living practices that must be enacted — resonant with the core insight that the via negativa is a via positiva ([[Structures of Persistence]]).

## Connection to the synthesis

Operative Traditions adds another dimension to the disciplinary foundation: the understanding that traditions themselves are structures of persistence, and that their operative (enacted, practiced, lived) character is what distinguishes living traditions from dead ones.

See also: [[Operative Traditions]], [[Structures of Persistence]], [[Formation]], [[Natural Law]]
