# Arthur Brock

Pioneer in [[Currency Design]] and [[Collective Intelligence]].

## Key contributions

- Currency design as a discipline — understanding "currency" as any formal system that shapes how value and information move, not limited to money
- Distributed systems thinking applied to human coordination
- Tools and frameworks for structuring flows of information, value, and obligation within groups

## Relationship to Statecraft Systems

One of the "masters of disciplines that the mainstream leaves out and often doesn't have words for" that Moritz studied with. Brock's work on currency design provides the architectural toolkit for structuring flows within the cooperative structures that [[Statecraft Systems]] helps build.

His influence is in the *how things flow* dimension — complementing [[Structural Dynamics]] (how things advance), [[Natural Law]] (what is viable), and [[Formation]] (how people are prepared).
