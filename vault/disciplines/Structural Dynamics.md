# Structural Dynamics

The engine at the center of everything [[Statecraft Systems]] does. Developed by [[Robert Fritz]].

Structural dynamics is a rigorous framework for understanding why some efforts advance toward their goals and others [[Oscillation|oscillate]]. It provides the methodology for the [[Creative Orientation]] and the mechanics of [[Structural Tension]].

## Core principles

1. **Structure determines behavior** — the underlying structure of a situation determines the path of least resistance. Change the structure, change the behavior.
2. **Creative vs. reactive** — the [[Creative Orientation]] and [[Reactive Orientation]] produce fundamentally different structural dynamics.
3. **Structural tension** — the discrepancy between desired outcome and current reality, when properly set up, creates a force that resolves toward the desired outcome.
4. **Success that succeeds** — outcomes that are structurally sound do not create conditions for their own reversal.
