# Systems

The structural element in [[Statecraft Systems]].

Systems are what remain after individuals leave. They compound. They hold long-term reciprocal relationships in place. A system either advances toward its intended outcomes or oscillates — and understanding why is the core of [[Structural Dynamics]].

"Systems" rather than "Studio," "Consulting," "Labs," or anything more conventional because:
- Systems are what remain and compound
- The word emphasizes the architectural and structural nature of the work
- It distances from the commoditized consulting model
- Things don't change fundamentally without new, coherent, viable *systems*

The impulse to make visible yet cosmetic changes is often what stands in the way of doing the real, necessary, and often boring or unpleasant structural work.

See also: [[Reciprocity]], [[Structural Tension]], [[Oscillation]]
