# Information Sovereignty

The capacity to process information correctly and quickly in a contested environment. A critical component of [[Sovereignty]] in the current moment.

## Why it matters now

The prevailing meta favors cognitive and information manipulation over kinetic engagement — it is generally cheaper. Psyops, misinformation, narrative warfare are the dominant tools of competition and control. Every piece of information has the potential to change orientation, belief, strategy, and action.

The speed requirement has increased dramatically. Think [[OODA Loop]] — getting inside someone else's decision cycle. The one who can orient and decide faster, based on better information processing, has the advantage.

## The AI dimension

AI tools provide information processing capacity at scales and speeds previously impossible. This makes [[AI-Native vs AI-Naive]] capability directly relevant to sovereignty — the AI-native operator can process, evaluate, and act on information flows that would overwhelm the AI-naive one.

## The structural dimension

Information sovereignty is not just about tools. It requires:
- [[Decidability]] — the ability to evaluate what is true
- [[Natural Law]] — the framework for seeing reality clearly
- Trusted relationships ([[The Web]]) — because no individual can verify everything alone
- Structural awareness — understanding how information flows shape behavior and outcomes
